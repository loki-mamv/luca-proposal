Pillar One

Platform

  • Ghost site redesign — Clean signup flow for invite-only Instagram, book promotion, and a content hub that actually drives traffic
  • Instagram launch — Invite-only model ($25 entry), curated feed with exclusive content not available anywhere else
  • LinkedIn presence — Professional forgiveness content for the audience that takes wellness seriously
  • "Ask the Book" chatbot — AI-powered tool that lets visitors explore forgiveness concepts conversationally, powered by the book itself

Each platform has a distinct job. Ghost attracts. Instagram converts. LinkedIn positions. The chatbot engages. No redundancy.

Pillar Two

Content engine

A weekly content calendar across all three channels, derived entirely from Luca's voice and the book. Nothing generic. Nothing that reads like a template.

  • Ghost — Ungated articles optimized for search. The reference library. What people find when they Google forgiveness practices.
  • Instagram — Exclusive content that justifies the $25 entry. Behind-the-scenes, deep dives, community Q&A.
  • LinkedIn — Thought leadership posts. The professional case for forgiveness in workplaces, relationships, leadership.

We create the drafts. You review and approve. Minimal time investment on your end — maximum consistency for your audience.
